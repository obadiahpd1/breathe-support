Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Center free to use?
Yes! The Center includes 4 breathing patterns completely free with no time limits. You can use Coherence Balance, Box Breathing, Resonance, and Deep Calm without ever paying. The Pro subscription unlocks all 9 patterns, the custom pattern builder, and more.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > The Center, then tap "Cancel Subscription." You'll still have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open The Center app on your new device, go to Profile > Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your Pro access will be restored automatically.
What breathing techniques are included?
The Center includes 9 science-backed breathing techniques: Coherence Balance, Box Breathing, 4-7-8 Relaxation, Vortex, Samadhi, Power Breath (Wim Hof inspired), Resonance, Deep Calm, and the Custom Pattern Builder. Each technique targets different goals like stress relief, focus, energy, or sleep.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! All breathing sessions work completely offline. You don't need an internet connection to practice. Your session history and streak data are stored locally on your device.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required. Simply download the app and start breathing right away. No sign-up, no email, no personal information needed.
How does the free trial work?
The Pro subscription includes a 7-day free trial. You get full access to all features during the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged. After the trial, it's $4.99/month.
I'm having a technical issue. What should I do?
First, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, make sure you're on the latest version by checking for updates in the App Store. If the problem continues, please email us at thecenterbreathwork@gmail.com with a description of the issue and your device model, and we'll help resolve it.
